Different Types of Forest Resources

Fruits

Fruits have excellent benefits for our health.They can reduce the risk of death almost by 42 per cent. The Journal of Epidemiology & Community Health has published the report online.This means, if you don’t want to visit the doctor’s clinic every now and then, you must develop the habit of eating fruits for encouraging an optimum health.

Wood

Wood really does grow on—or rather in—trees. Who'd have thought you could make a lovely coffee table or a fruit bowl from a gnarled old specimen like this? The outer part of a tree trunk might look dead, but it's very much alive: tree trunks grow outward (getting wider) as well as upward (getting higher).

Honey

Honey was almost the only source of sugar available to the ancients and was valued for its medicinal benefits. It was used to make mead, a fermented beverage, and was mixed with wine and other alcoholic drinks.

Herbal Medicines

What is herbal medicine good for? Herbal medicine is used to treat many conditions, such as allergies, asthma, eczema, premenstrual syndrome, rheumatoid arthritis, fibromyalgia, migraine, menopausal symptoms, chronic fatigue, irritable bowel syndrome, and cancer, among others.

Paper

Paper is a versatile product that is created most often from wood pulp, usually derived from softwood coniferous trees such as pine. Coniferous trees are fast-growing trees that are characterized as having cones and needles as opposed to deciduous trees which have leaves that are shed during the autumn and winter seasons

Oxygen

O2 is a very important part of respiration. Because of this, it is used in medicine. It is used to increase the amount of oxygen in a persons blood so more respiration can take place. This can make them become healthy quicker if they are ill. Oxygen therapy is used to treat emphysema, pneumonia, some heart problems, and any disease that makes it harder for a person to take in oxygen.

Conclusion

As we have all seen, forests give us a lot of benefits but in recent times a lot of trees are being cuted indiscriminantky and we should ban such practices and protect trees.
